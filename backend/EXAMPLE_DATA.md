- `BAGS`

- `URGENT`

- `IDENTITY_RELATED`
- `MEDICAL`
- `CHILD_RELATED`

**Total Possible Combinations**: 2 (statuses) × 11 (categories) × 11 (tags) = **242 combinations**

## Tips for Choosing the Right Category and Tag

### Category Selection Guide
1. **ELECTRONICS** - Phones, tablets, laptops, cameras, headphones
2. **DOCUMENTS** - Passports, IDs, papers, certificates, tickets
3. **ACCESSORIES** - Wallets, watches, sunglasses, belts, ties
4. **CLOTHING** - Jackets, hats, scarves, gloves, shoes
5. **JEWELRY** - Rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings
6. **BAGS** - Backpacks, purses, briefcases, luggage
7. **KEYS** - Car keys, house keys, office keys
8. **PETS** - Dogs, cats, birds, any animals
9. **SPORTS_EQUIPMENT** - Bikes, balls, rackets, gear
10. **BOOKS** - Textbooks, notebooks, journals
11. **OTHER** - Everything else

### Tag Selection Guide
1. **URGENT** - Time-sensitive, needs immediate attention
2. **REWARD_OFFERED** - Owner offering reward for return
3. **SENTIMENTAL_VALUE** - Emotionally valuable (heirlooms, gifts)
4. **HIGH_VALUE** - Expensive or monetarily valuable
5. **PERISHABLE** - Food items or time-sensitive materials
6. **FRAGILE** - Delicate items that need careful handling
7. **IDENTITY_RELATED** - Contains personal identification
8. **MEDICAL** - Medical devices, prescriptions, health-related
9. **CHILD_RELATED** - Children's items or toys
10. **ELECTRONICS_TAG** - Use with ELECTRONICS category for priority
11. **VEHICLE_RELATED** - Car-related documents or keys
